# company_brief - CEVA Logistics

## About the Company

- **Sector**: Contract Logistics & Freight Management (global 3PL)
- **Parent**: CMA CGM Group (acquired CEVA in 2019)
- **Scale**: ~98,000 employees, operations in 170+ countries, revenue ~€17B
- **HQ**: Marseille (Group) / Baar, Switzerland (CEVA operational HQ)
- **Subsidiaries include**: Colis Privé (B2C parcel, France), MNC (air freight), freight forwarding divisions

## Contract Logistics Division

- One of the top 5 global contract logistics providers
- Sectors served: E-Commerce, Healthcare, Retail, Aerospace, Automotive, Food & Beverage, Technology
- Key capability: Zero Defect Start Up (ZDS) - standardised methodology for launching new client contracts with zero operational defects
- Systems: WMS (multiple platforms), OMS, Dock Appointment Systems, Optymee (internal optimisation tool)
- Current push: AI insertion, robotics, automation across warehousing operations

## Strategic Priorities 2025-2026

- Scaling ZDS methodology globally - consistency across all new contract launches
- Technology insertion: AI, robotics, WMS upgrades
- Integration and optimisation post-CMA CGM acquisition
- Multi-sector diversification and key account onboarding acceleration

## Why This Role is Strategic

The Global Contract Logistics Program & System Director owns the ZDS function globally - ensuring every new client contract launches without operational defects. In a business where new contracts represent revenue growth and failed launches represent client attrition and reputational risk, this is a high-stakes, high-visibility role. The person in this position sets the standard for how CEVA onboards clients at scale.
