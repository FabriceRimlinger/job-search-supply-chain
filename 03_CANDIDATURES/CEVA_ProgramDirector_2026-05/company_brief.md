**Date**: 2026-05-08

## 1. Overview

**CEVA Logistics** is a global third-party logistics (3PL) company headquartered in Baar, Switzerland.
- Revenue: ~18.3 billion USD (2025) - stable vs. 2024
- EBITDA: 1.7 billion USD (margin ~9.4%)
- Operations: 160+ countries, 110,000+ employees, 800+ contract logistics sites, 11M+ sqm warehouse space

**Parent company**: CMA CGM Group (fully owned subsidiary since 2019)
- CMA CGM 2025 revenue: 54.4 billion USD
- CEVA is the logistics arm of one of the world's largest shipping groups

**Business lines**:
- Contract Logistics (CL): warehouse management, fulfillment, value-added services
- Freight Management: air, ocean, ground, customs brokerage

- Covers E-Commerce, Healthcare, Consumer Retail, Aerospace, Food, Technology, Automotive - multi-vertical

## 3. Strategic Priorities 2025-2026

### Manhattan Active WMS / OMS Rollout (announced Oct 2025)
CEVA is executing a multi-year phased deployment of Manhattan Associates' Active Warehouse Management (WMS) and Order Management (OMS) systems across its global CL network. The program covers 50+ sites in North America first, then Europe and other global locations. When complete, Manhattan systems will run at more than half of CEVA's CL sites, with remaining sites on CEVA's proprietary Matrix WMS.

This is the direct operational context for the Global CL Program & System Director role. The role governs:
- Zero Defect Start Up (ZDS) methodology for new customer onboarding
- Global project implementation (Optymee, Dock Appointment System/DAS)
- Infinity Loop strategic improvement cycles
- Legacy WMS transitions and CL efficiency systems

### Zero Defect Start Up (ZDS)
CEVA's proprietary customer onboarding framework: a dedicated team of experts covers all customer requirements at launch - project management, budgetary discipline, service quality from Day 1. The Global Director role owns this function globally and leads the ZDS Community across all regions.

### Acquisitions and Expansion
- Acquisition of Borusan Lojistik (Turkey) - expanding CL footprint in EMEA
- Agreement to acquire Fagioli Group (Italy) - project logistics and heavy lift [Mar 2026]
- New eCommerce warehouse in Dubai [Jan 2026]

### Sustainability
- Sustainable road transport pilot (Avolta partnership, HVO fuel, Duo Trailer)
- Lithium-ion battery reverse logistics (new maritime service, Mar 2026)
- CMA CGM 2025 Sustainability Report published Mar 2026

## 4. CMA CGM / CEVA Group Structure

CMA CGM is a Franco-Monegasque shipping group founded in 1978 by Jacques Saade, now led by Rodolphe Saade (Chairman & CEO). CMA CGM acquired CEVA in 2019, creating a fully integrated shipping-to-logistics group.

**Colis Privé**: French last-mile parcel delivery company, majority-owned by CMA CGM / CEVA. Fabrice worked at Colis Privé from 2023 to 2026. This provides direct insider knowledge of group CL operating standards - a genuine differentiator for this application.
