## Role Summary

Lead and scale JD Logistics' last-mile delivery operations across France — urban, suburban and rural. Full ownership of delivery station operations (sortation, dispatch), team leadership, compliance, safety and budget management.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and implement nationwide last-mile delivery strategy across all geographies
- Lead end-to-end delivery station operations including sortation and dispatch
- Build and develop high-performance leadership teams
- Ensure compliance with French labour laws and transport regulations
- Conduct safety audits and implement continuous improvement initiatives
- Manage operational budgets and resource planning

## Key Requirements

- Bachelor's in Logistics, Supply Chain or Business Administration (Master's preferred)
- 7+ years high-volume logistics/transportation, 3+ years senior management
- Proven experience managing **200+ employee teams**
- Deep understanding of **French labour law** and transport compliance
- Fluency in French and English — **Mandarin preferred**
