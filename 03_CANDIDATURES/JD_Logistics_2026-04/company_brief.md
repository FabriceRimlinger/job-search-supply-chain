# company_brief - JD.com / JD Logistics

## About the Company

- **Parent**: JD.com (JingDong) - China's largest B2C e-commerce company by revenue (~€115B, 2023)
- **Entity**: JD Logistics - separately listed since 2021 (HKEX: 2618)
- **Scale**: ~350,000 employees globally, operations across China and expanding internationally
- **Model**: asset-heavy, own-fleet logistics (vs. marketplace model) - quality and speed as differentiators
- **Known for**: same-day/next-day delivery in China, extreme operational standards, tech-first approach (drones, autonomous vehicles, smart warehouses)

## European Expansion

- JD Logistics has been building European infrastructure since 2021 - warehouse hub in the Netherlands, expansion into France, Germany, UK
- Strategy: replicate in Europe what Amazon built in 2012-2018, with JD's own operational standards
- France is a priority market - significant e-commerce volume, complex last-mile landscape
- The France Head of Last Mile role is essentially a **founding role** - building the network from zero

## Culture & Operating Style

- **Tech-first**: data, automation, real-time tracking are core to everything
- **Performance-driven**: extremely high standards, fast pace - similar to Amazon culture but with Chinese corporate intensity
- **Long-term horizon**: JD invests heavily before profitability - willing to build infrastructure for scale
- **Mandarin-preferred** for senior roles: reflects internal working language and HQ relationships - not a dealbreaker for Western hires but an advantage

## Last-Mile Strategy

- Own delivery stations rather than pure asset-light DSP model
- Investment in technology: route optimisation, real-time tracking, automated sortation
- Focus on quality differentiation vs. existing players (La Poste, DPD, Colissimo)
- Compliance with French labour law is a known challenge for Chinese companies entering France - major operational risk requiring experienced local leadership

## Why This Role is Strategic

This is a founding role. The person hired will design and build JD's French last-mile network from the ground up - station locations, operational model, DSP strategy, compliance framework, technology stack. High visibility at Group level, direct line to JD Logistics international leadership.
